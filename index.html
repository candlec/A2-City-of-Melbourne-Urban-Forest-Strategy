City of Melbourne Urban Forest Strategy

The city of Melbourne aims to have a tree canopy of 40% by 2040

The map to the right has a dot for each tree placement in the City of Melbourne from the year 2019 in pink and 2021 in green. There were 114784 trees recorded in 2019 and 57980 trees recorded in 2021, that is a loss of more than half the tree popuation over 3 years
